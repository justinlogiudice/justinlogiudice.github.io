IoTerra: The Smart Planting Solution 🌳

IoTerra is an Internet of Things project developed by Justin Lo Giudice 👨‍💻

A third-year Computer Engineering student at McMaster University in Hamilton, ON, Canada 🍁


Its name is derived from the Roman goddess of the Earth: Terra, and stands for the Internet of Terra, or Internet of the Earth, which is precisely was this project was designed to do:

Create a link between our Earth and possibly the greatest human invention of all time: the Internet! 🌎↔️🌐
